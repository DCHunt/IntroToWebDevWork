AC/DC

BACK IN BLACK

JULY 25 1980

Back in Black is the seventh studio album by Australian rock band AC/DC. Produced by Robert John "Mutt" Lange, the album was released on 25 July 1980.

BAND MEMBERS


Brian Johnson - Lead Vocals

Angus Young - Lead Guitar

Malcom Young - Rythm Guitar

Cliff Williams - Bass Guitar

Phil Rudd - Drums
